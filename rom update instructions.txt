If there is a problem with the use, you can update the rom

romV1.8

Upgrade method:

Rename the rom1.8 in the directory to update.app
Connect the turzx screen, click the setting button
Click the refresh storage information button to ensure that the turzx screen is connected normally
In the case of ensuring that the connection is normal, click the upgrade rom button, wait for the prompt that the transfer is successful, and then click to restart the turzx screen to complete the upgrade.
